# Requirements Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before turning fuzzy requirements into an AE plan.

- [ ] Problem frame states WHAT and WHY before HOW.
- [ ] Users or affected systems are named, or explicitly not applicable.
- [ ] Functional requirements are behavior-focused.
- [ ] Acceptance criteria are testable or inspectable.
- [ ] Non-goals and scope boundaries are explicit.
- [ ] Assumptions are separated from confirmed facts.
- [ ] Critical ambiguities are answered or recorded as open questions.
- [ ] Validation signals are named for each acceptance criterion.
- [ ] Risks and constraints are visible enough for planning.
- [ ] The scope is small enough for one implementation plan, or split criteria are documented.
